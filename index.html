Who
Am I?

Content Writer & Strategist

I'm a Gen-Z content writer who has spent 21 years of her life as a customer. Which basically means I know exactly what the person reaching out to your brand wants to feel.

I've learned that people never buy products, services, or ideas. They buy solutions, emotions, and outcomes.

I understand audience pain points and strike where it matters most. I know how to position your product or service as a necessity to your audience.

With a background in finance, I bring analytical thinking into every piece I write to create an impact, build trust, and convert readers into loyal customers.
